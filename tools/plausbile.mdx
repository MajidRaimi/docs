### Why Plausible Over Google Analytics? ü§î

While Google Analytics is a powerful tool, Plausible offers several advantages that make it a better fit for our needs:

- **Privacy-Focused**: Plausible is designed with user privacy in mind. It does not track personal data or use cookies, making it compliant with privacy regulations like GDPR.
- **Open Source**: As an open-source platform, Plausible allows us to self-host and customize our analytics setup to match our specific needs.
- **Simple and Clean UI**: Plausible offers a clean and intuitive interface that provides all the essential analytics without the complexity and clutter often found in other analytics tools.
- **Actionable Insights**: With Plausible, we get straightforward and actionable insights into our product's performance, helping us make data-driven decisions without getting lost in endless metrics.
